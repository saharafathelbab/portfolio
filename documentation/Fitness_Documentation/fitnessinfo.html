Fitness: Workouts & Nutrition

  • Why was this project created?

  • A friend of mine struggled to be consistent with working out at the Montclair State University (MSU) Recreational Center due to a hectic schedule as well as with incorporating healthy meals in her daily life. After observing this problem, I decided to develop a solution.

  • Aim of Fitness Project:

  • The aim of this Fitness Project is to be a resource where my friend will be able to follow along with a YouTube Personal Trainer for short workouts and select easy to make healthy recipes for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

    As more of an add-on, I included a page of suggested videos to watch as well as books and articles to read, based on her interests, to emphasize the importance of Recovery Days.







Special Note:

After completing this project for my one friend, three other friends also found the idea of it brilliant and currently use this to maintain a healthy diet and workout schedule.

What is the problem and how does this Fitness Project solve it?

Problem: A friend of mine struggled to be consistent with working out at the Montclair State University (MSU) Recreational Center due to a hectic schedule as well as with incorporating healthy meals in her daily life.

Fitness: Workouts & Nutrition Project Solution: This Fitness: Workouts & Nutrition Project provides my friend with a selection of workouts from a Personal Trainer that vary in times to fit in her hectic schecule, a selection of breakfast, lunch and dinner ideas, and a collection of Bloggers & YouTubers I currated for her based on her interests.

I would like to note that I am currently redesigning & developing this project. I created this project in January 2018 and I have grown since then and would like to provide my friend with a refined version.

How did you decide on what workout videos to include?

My first question for my friend was: What area of your body would you like the workout the most?

She responded that she wanted to tone her arms and legs. From there, I decided what workout videos to include from the Personal Trainer on Youtube, Lucy Wyndham-Read.

Did you prototype your pages before coding?

At this point in my coding journey, January 2018, I was not introduced to the idea of prototyping. I created sketches in my notepad, which I later learned was referred to as Wireframing, of how I wanted the pages to look like.

What is a Recovery Day?

A recovery day is a day for your body to rest. Working out is important, and allowing your body to recover is just as important.

For this page, the general idea was to have a collection of suggested articles and videos for the user to watch. After asking my friend what she preferred to watch and read in her free time, I selected articles, books and YouTube videos that correlated with her response.
