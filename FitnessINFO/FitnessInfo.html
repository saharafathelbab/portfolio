Fitness & Nutrition

A friend of mine struggled to be consistent with working out at the Montclair State University (MSU) Recreational Center due to a hectic schedule as well as with incorporating healthy meals in her daily life. As a result, I decided to compose a place where she will be able to follow along with a YouTube Personal Trainer for short workouts and select easy to make healthy recipes for breakfast, lunch and dinner. As more of an add-on, I included a page of suggested videos to watch as well as books and articles to read, based on her interests, to emphasize the importance of Recovery Days.
