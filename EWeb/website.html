Comfy Vibes

For my Internet Computing class, as a Final Project my partner and I decided to create the E-Commerce Website: Comfy Vibes. The E-Commerce Website we created centralizes on Men's Apparel with a selection of Shoes and Jackets for women.With our wide variety of clothing from casualwear to eveningwear we not only aimed to serve customers with the utmost quality of our line of clothing but to provide and make our customers feel that they have without a doubt, received only the best!

What software did I use to create this project?

Atom

How did my partner and I code this project?

HTML & CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL Database

__________

Who is meant to shop at this website?

Men and Women

What is available for Men?

Jackets, Sweaters, Jeans, Shoes

What is available for Women?

Jackets and Shoes

Once the user comes on ComfyVibes, they are greeted with a JavaScript pop up.
The formation of the Home Page is clockwise.

For the header of the Home Page, the user is able to click the 'VISIT US' button to be taken to About & Location of the store. For the navigation section, when the user selects either Men, Women,Shoes or Sale they are taken to that page.

The first section of the Home Page is a one-line-grid-format of the Men's Section categories. The user is able to click the 'Shop Now!' to be taken to the page of Men's Sweaters, Jeans or Jackets.

The bottom section of the Home Page directs the user - upon clicking on the 'Shop Now!' to the Sale Section. The footer of the Home Page contains a link to the About, Return Policy and Contact Information.
