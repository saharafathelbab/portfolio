Take Me to Thailand

My team and I developed a restaurant website, Take Me to Thailand, which allowed customers to view the items on the menu and place an order accordingly. The customer had to have a valid login which was encrypted in the database for security purposes. Upon logging in, the customer was able to view the PDF form of the menu before making the decision to begin their order and to select if they would like to dine in or takeout. A teammate and I were tasked with creating four views: Employee, Customer, Owner, Manager where the login info entered would direct them to the page(s) they were allowed to access.

Login

  • Returning/New Customer

    _____

    If the person is a returning customer,
    they proceed to type in their details,
    username and password. If, however, they are a new customer to Take Me to Thailand they tap Register on the bottom left corner.

  • Employee/Owner/Manager

    _____

    Based on the login details entered, the system would know which page they should be directed to.

    Employee: They are able to see other employees and Managers.

    Owner: The Owner is able to View/Add/Remove Managers Add/Remove items from the menu, View/Add Employees.

    Manager: Add/Remove items from the menu.

Home Page

_____

When the customer logs in, they are taken to the homepage, on the left hand side. The two options below the slideshow of meals are "Start Order" and "View Menu". When the customer taps on "View Menu" they are taken to the menu of Take Me to Thailand in PDF format, as shown in the image on the right. Take Me to Thailand has Appetizers, Soup, Lunch, Dinner and Drinks.

Start Order

_____

When the customer taps on Start Order they are taken to the above image on the left hand side. After tapping Start Order to confirm that they are ready to start their order, the ordering system of Take Me to Thailand then proceeds to ask them if they are going to Dine In or Take Out their meal.

Menu

_____

The menu on mobile is in two columns as shown in the above image on the left hand side. When the user click on View item they are shown the item they clicked in format on the right hand side.

Cart

_____

When the customer places one item, as shown in the photo on the left hand side, they also have the option to continue browsing the menu or to proceed to check out. On the right hand side is when the customer adds more to their cart. At the top, they are able to empty cart and start fresh if they feel they need to start over. If they would like to remove an item from their cart, they click the X in the Remove section.

In the Description of the item, it allows the customer to view how many rewards points they would be receiving if they ordered what they've chosen. They also have the option to change the quantity while viewing their cart.

At the end of the page, they view their total dollar amount and total reward points they would earn from their cart.

Checkout

_____

On the left hand side, the customer essentialy clarifies their name, or put the name of the person who will be picking up the meal at the Take Me to Thailand Restaurant. They then select their form of payment.

After tapping Complete Order they are then shown their reciept on the right hand side.
